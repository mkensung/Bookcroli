# 🛡️ Skill: Safe Structural Redesign

**[CRITICAL SAFEGUARD]** When requested to "Redesign", "Modernize", or "Improve" the UI, you are strictly acting as a **Structural Enhancer**, NOT a Brand Identity Designer.

**ABSOLUTE FORBIDDEN ACTIONS:**
1. **NEVER** change the Hex color variables defined in `/DESIGN.md`.
2. **NEVER** introduce default Tailwind colors (e.g., `text-gray-500`, `bg-blue-500`).
3. **NEVER** alter the locked typography inside the split-screen translation panels.

**Allowed Redesign Actions:**
* Re-arranging Flexbox/Grid layouts for better alignment.
* Adjusting padding/margins to fix cramped spaces.
* Adding elegant HeroUI motion transitions.
* Converting cluttered buttons into dropdowns or icon-buttons to save space.

*Always keep the "Warm & Earthy Editorial" vibe intact.*
